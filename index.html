About

Working at the intersection of GIS, agriculture, and education.

I'm a high school senior dually enrolled at Wake Technical Community College, heading to NC State this fall. I lead the National 4-H Geospatial Leadership Team — now active in three states — and run The Map Company by Estevez, a GIS consulting practice focused on market intelligence, environmental data tools, and outreach visualization for nonprofits and small businesses.

My work centers on one question: how can we use data and spatial storytelling to build more just, connected communities? I've partnered with city agencies, rural stakeholders, and K–12 systems to design tools that make policy problems visible — and solvable.

Long term, I'm building toward a multidisciplinary firm spanning sustainable real estate, rail infrastructure, GIS consulting, agribusiness innovation, and workforce development — with a focus on rural America and Latin America.
