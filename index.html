About

I'm a high school senior completing an Associate in Arts at Wake Technical Community College, heading to NC State this fall to study Agricultural Business Management. I lead the National 4-H Geospatial Leadership Team and run The Map Company by Estevez, a GIS consulting business focused on market intelligence, environmental data tools, and outreach visualization for nonprofits and small businesses.

My work sits at the intersection of geospatial science, agricultural systems, and civic technology. I develop GIS curriculum for K-12 students in Wake County Public Schools, build tools that help communities visualize food access and environmental impacts, and teach youth how to use spatial data to solve real-world problems.

I'm interested in sustainable rural infrastructure, geospatial pedagogy, and using maps as instruments for community empowerment.
